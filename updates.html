What Happens Next

  1. 1Submit this form with your contact details
  2. 2We verify your license and prepare your update package
  3. 3We email you the .zip file within 24 hours
  4. 4Go to System → Updates in SentinelVision, upload the file, and you're done
  5. 5The system applies the update and restarts automatically

How to Apply the Update

  • Navigate to Updates in the sidebar
  • Drag & drop or browse for the .zip file we sent
  • Click Upload & Apply Update
  • The system restarts automatically — your data is always preserved
Your data is safe: Updates only replace application code. All cameras, recordings, settings and detections remain completely intact.
