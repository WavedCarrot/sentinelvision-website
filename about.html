About

Built to Give You Real Security

Not just recordings — actual intelligence that keeps watch while you get on with your life.

Why We Built SentinelVision

Like many South Africans, we got tired of finding out about break-ins, theft, and incidents only after the fact — scrolling back through hours of footage to piece together what happened. Cloud-based AI camera services existed, but they meant paying R200–R500 per camera per month to a foreign company, trusting your footage to servers in another country, and depending on a stable internet connection for your security system to function.

We knew there had to be a better way. So we built it. SentinelVision Pro runs entirely on a small local device — no cloud, no footage leaving your network, no per-camera subscription. State-of-the-art YOLO AI models run on your own hardware and send you an instant Telegram photo alert the second something happens, whether your internet is up or not.

What started as a personal project for a single property has grown into a fully-featured platform now running across retail shops, warehouses, residential complexes, and small businesses across South Africa. Every feature in SentinelVision has been shaped by real-world deployments — the zones, the false-alert button, the per-schedule arming, the animal detection thresholds — all built because a real user needed it.

We're a small South African software team. That means local pricing in ZAR, local support with real response times, and a genuine understanding of the security challenges facing South African property owners. When you contact us, you speak to the same developer who wrote the code.

What We Stand For

Our Core Principles

Every decision — from how we price licenses to how we architect the software — comes back to these.

Privacy First

Your video footage never leaves your network. All AI processing runs locally on your hardware. We have no access to your cameras, recordings or alerts.

Accessible to Everyone

Enterprise-level AI security should not cost enterprise prices. We price in ZAR, keep licenses affordable, and never charge per camera or per alert.

Continuous Improvement

SentinelVision is actively maintained and improved. New features, bug fixes and performance improvements are released regularly and delivered via one-click in-app updates.

Security by Design

Secure login, encrypted sessions, rate limiting, complete event audit logs and rollback protection are all built in — not bolted on as an afterthought.

Works Offline

Internet cuts are a reality in South Africa. SentinelVision keeps detecting and logging events even without internet — Telegram alerts resume automatically when connectivity returns.

Real Support

When you email us or message on WhatsApp, you get a response from someone who actually built the software — not a generic support ticket. We typically reply within a few hours.

Our Mission

To make intelligent, proactive security accessible to every South African property owner — without cloud lock-in, without complexity, and without breaking the budget.

A camera that only records is a witness after the fact. SentinelVision makes your cameras act before the damage is done.
