The Problem We Were Living With

Break-ins. Armed robberies. Stock walkouts. Trespassing at 2am. As South Africans, these aren't hypotheticals — they're Tuesday. And for years the only options were a reactive CCTV recorder that you'd review after something went wrong, or an expensive cloud AI service charging R200–R500 per camera per month to a foreign company — while your footage sat on their servers.

Neither was acceptable. So in 2024 we started building SentinelVision Pro: AI that runs entirely on a small local device in your building, watches every camera in real time, and fires a Telegram photo alert to your phone the instant something happens. No cloud dependency. No per-camera fees. No footage leaving your network.

From One Property to Many

The first deployment was a farm on the outskirts of Bloemfontein. Within weeks it had caught multiple after-hours perimeter breaches and unauthorised vehicle access. Word spread. Deployments followed in warehouses, retail shops, residential complexes, and small businesses across Southern Africa.

Every feature you see today came from a real deployment gap — the custom detection zones were added when a client needed to watch a specific gate without triggering on the road behind it. The per-species animal detection thresholds came from a farm where the system kept alerting on cattle. The offline-queue for Telegram alerts was added after Eskom proved, again, that you cannot trust the internet to always be there. This is a platform shaped entirely by real-world use.

Use Cases

Who Runs SentinelVision

The same software adapts to the specific detection needs of every site.

Retail & Spaza Shops

After-hours intrusion alerts, stock theft detection, and people counting during trading hours. Real-time Telegram alerts mean you know about it before the thief is gone.

Warehouses & Factories

Zone-based perimeter monitoring with scheduled arming so the system only alerts when the site should be empty. Knife and weapon detection on high-risk areas.

Residential Complexes

Loitering detection at gates, vehicle monitoring, and shared alert channels for body corporates. No cloud means no POPIA headaches around storing resident footage externally.

Residential Homes

Instant photo alerts to your phone when someone enters your driveway or garden — even during a power cut. Works with almost any existing IP or RTSP camera.

Farms & Smallholdings

Per-species animal detection thresholds prevent constant alerts from your own livestock, while still catching vehicles or people in restricted areas at night.

Restaurants & Hospitality

After-hours kitchen and stock room monitoring, staff area access control, and queue-length people counting to improve customer flow during peak hours.

What We Stand For

Our Core Principles

From how we price licenses to how we architect the software — every decision comes back to these.

Privacy First

Your footage never leaves your network. All AI processing happens on-site. We have zero access to your cameras, recordings, or alerts — by design, not just by policy.

Accessible to Everyone

Enterprise-grade AI security should not cost enterprise prices. Single lifetime license fee, priced in ZAR. No per-camera charges, no monthly subscriptions, no surprise bills.

Always Improving

SentinelVision ships updates regularly — features, fixes, and performance improvements delivered via one-click in-app updates. You stay current without lifting a finger.

Security by Design

Secure login, rate limiting, encrypted sessions, full event audit logs, and rollback-safe updates are baked in — not retrofitted after the fact.

Load-Shedding Resilient

System draws under 10W on low-power hardware — trivial to keep alive on a small UPS. Telegram alerts queue offline and flush automatically when connectivity returns.

Real People. Real Support.

Email us or message on WhatsApp and you'll hear back from the developer who built the feature you're asking about — usually within a couple of hours, not a couple of days.

Our Mission

To make intelligent, proactive security accessible to every South African property owner — without cloud lock-in, without complexity, and without breaking the budget.
