Setup

Up and Running in Minutes

No IT team needed. No cloud account. Just plug it in and follow three steps.

1

Connect to Your Network

Plug the unit into your router with the included LAN cable. It gets an IP address automatically.

2

Add Your Cameras

Open the web dashboard on any device on your network and add your cameras using their RTSP stream address.

3

Configure Alerts

Link your Telegram account, draw detection zones, set your arm schedule, and you're protected.
